## Faculty

<div class="faculty-section">

### XuGuo SHI

**Shixuguo** is male, from Tai'an, Shandong Province, China. He is currently an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor. His research focuses on spaceborne radar interferometry technology and its applications. He received his Ph.D. in Engineering from the State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing (LIESMARS), Wuhan University in 2016. He has undertaken and participated in several national research projects including the National Natural Science Foundation of China and the National 973 Program. He has published over 20 SCI-indexed papers and co-authored one monograph. He has served as a reviewer for several international SCI journals including *ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing*, *Remote Sensing*, *Landslides*, and *Science of The Total Environment*.

His research interests mainly focus on high-precision deformation monitoring methods using radar interferometry, landslide geological hazard identification and monitoring, and related applications in geohazard early warning systems.
