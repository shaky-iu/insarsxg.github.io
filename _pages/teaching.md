### Office Hours
- **Time:** Tuesdays & Thursdays, 14:00-16:00
- **Location:** Room 508, Geoscience Building
- **Format:** Both in-person and online (Zoom)
- **Appointment:** Recommended via email

### Research Opportunities
We welcome motivated students to join our research projects. Current openings include:

1. **Research Assistant Positions** (2 openings)
   - Requirements: Strong programming skills (Python/MATLAB)
   - Duration: 1 year minimum
   - Support: Monthly stipend provided

2. **Summer Internships** (4-6 positions annually)
   - For: Undergraduate and Master's students
   - Projects: SAR data processing, algorithm development
   - Outcome: Potential for conference papers

3. **Visiting Students**
   - From: Other universities in China and abroad
   - Duration: 3-12 months
   - Support: Accommodation and living allowance

### Thesis Proposal Guidelines
Students planning to work with our group should:
1. Review our recent publications
2. Identify a specific research gap
3. Develop preliminary methodology
4. Contact Prof. Shixuguo with a 1-page proposal
