   License Period: 2022-2027  
   Application: Urban infrastructure safety monitoring

   Licensed to: China Coal Research Institute  
   License Period: 2021-2026

   Version: V2.5 (Released: 2023-08)  
   Deployment: 15 provincial geological environment monitoring stations
